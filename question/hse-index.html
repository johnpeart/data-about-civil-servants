Proxy stress index

A measure of wellbeing. A lower result represents a greater capacity to prevent and manage stress in a team. Questions B05, B08, B18, B26, B30, B33, B45 and E03.

For each respondent, a proxy stress score is calculated as the average score across the eight questions. The index is the average score of all respondents.
